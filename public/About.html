About us:

Synapsai is an innovative AI platform designed to provide users with a seamless and intuitive experience. Created by Bautista Perez, a 16-year-old programmer and game developer, Synapsai was born in La Pampa, Argentina, on April 18, 2025.

The platform is built using a variety of technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python. Synapsai aims to offer a free version that will always be accessible to users, ensuring that everyone can benefit from its advanced AI capabilities.

With Synapsai, users can engage in conversations about various topics, such as programming, nature, and more. The AI is powered by GPT-4 Mini, allowing for dynamic and non-default responses.

As Synapsai continues to grow, we plan to implement several updates, including enhanced response generation, improved fluidity, and interface improvements. Our goal is to make Synapsai the go-to platform for AI interactions.
